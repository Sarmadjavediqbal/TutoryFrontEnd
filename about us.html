About Us

Tutory.pk is an online platform for bringing students and tutors together for tuition sessions. We believe in providing quality education to our students and that is why we have a wide range of qualified tutors which the student or their family can choose for tuition sessions through the comfort of their homes.

The idea came after identifying gaps in our educational framework where a lot of students had a problem with convenience/time/money to pursue tuition sessions. We have qualitative and quantitative feedback check on our website for quality assurance and our wide array of tools in 1-1 tuition sessions allow students and tutors to have an interactive learning process.
